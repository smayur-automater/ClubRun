# ClubRuns — Market, Competitors, UVP (Phases 1–3)

Owner: Marketing agent, approved by CEO. Product reset 2026-07-01 — this supersedes all prior "ClubRun club-admin SaaS" positioning.

## Phase 1 — Market

Running is the largest participation sport on earth and still growing post-2020: parkrun alone clears 350k+ weekly finishers, major-marathon ballots are oversubscribed 4–10×, and run-club attendance among 18–34s has become a mainstream social activity (widely reported as "running is the new nightclub"). Three structural shifts create the opening:

1. **Running went social.** Gen Z/Millennials join clubs for belonging first, fitness second. The unit of growth is the *crew*, not the individual athlete.
2. **The tooling didn't follow.** Crews coordinate in WhatsApp/Instagram DMs and track in Strava — two disconnected worlds. Organizers hand-roll spreadsheets for attendance and pace groups.
3. **Beginners are the biggest cohort and the least served.** Existing apps assume you already run; nobody owns "I want to start running with people."

Target segments, in order: (1) urban social run crews and their members, (2) beginners looking for a crew, (3) club organizers, then later corporates/universities/charities/race organizers per AGENTS.md's long-term list.

## Phase 2 — Competitors

| Player | What it owns | Where it's weak for us |
|---|---|---|
| Strava | Post-run tracking + async kudos feed | Clubs are a bolted-on afterthought; no pre-run coordination, no pace groups, no attendance; intimidating to beginners |
| Nike Run Club | Solo guided runs, polished brand | Fundamentally single-player; no community layer at all |
| Garmin/Coros ecosystems | Hardware-locked data depth | No social organization layer; enthusiast-only |
| Heylo / Meetup | Group event logistics | Generic events tooling — no running features (pace, routes, tracking, streaks) |
| Runna / training-plan apps | Structured coaching | Solo again; a plan, not a crew |
| WhatsApp/Instagram (the real incumbent) | Where crews actually live today | Zero structure: RSVPs, pace groups, routes, safety, attendance all manual |

Nobody owns the intersection of *organizing group runs* and *tracking them*. That intersection is the product.

## Phase 3 — Unique Value Proposition

**ClubRuns: never run alone.**

The only running app built around the group run as the atomic unit — find your crew, RSVP to the run, show up, track it together, and keep the streak alive with people who notice when you don't show.

Positioning against each incumbent in one line:
- vs Strava: Strava records what you ran; ClubRuns gets you to the start line.
- vs NRC: coaching for one vs. momentum from many.
- vs WhatsApp: structure (RSVPs, pace groups, routes, check-ins) without losing the group-chat soul.

Defensibility: social lock-in. Leaving Strava costs you a graph of kudos; leaving ClubRuns costs you your Tuesday-night crew. Highest-retention moat available in fitness.
